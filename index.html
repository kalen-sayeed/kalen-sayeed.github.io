Recipe #1

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 2-4 humans
Ingredients Quantity
Food 1 1 lb(s)
Food 2 1 box of spaghetti or as much as desired
Food 3 1 jar of sauce

Step 1: Cook food 1 to 178 degrees farhenhiet
Step 2: Boil water then add food 2, cook for 7-8 minutes, strain when finished
Step 3: Add food 3 to food 1 to heat
Step 4: Top food 2 with food 1 and enjoy!
